Founder story

Built by someone who takes this work personally.

I'm Sergey, a personal transformation teacher based in Amsterdam. I am building The Awakening as a live online experience for people who are tired of carrying everything alone and ready to experience presence in a real, lived way.

This first version is intentionally simple. I am reading every survey response myself so the program can be shaped around what people are actually carrying, not around guesswork.

What this is

A small, live program for people ready for a real shift.

The Awakening is designed as a 5–6 week live online program that combines breathwork, deep meditation, and intentional community. It is made for people who do not need more information. They need a way back to themselves.

What this is not

  • Not another productivity framework dressed up as healing.
  • Not a self-serve app you try to figure out alone.
  • Not a course built without listening to the people it is meant for.

The Inner Rewrite, in plain language

  1. Slow down enough to feel what is real. Use breath and attention to come out of constant pressure and into the truth of what is happening inside you.
  2. See the patterns beneath the surface. Make space for the stress, numbness, and disconnection that have been running quietly in the background.
  3. Practice returning with other people. Build presence as a daily experience, held inside a live group rather than in isolation.

Who this is for

You will probably know if this is for you.

  • The old way of living has stopped working — you feel it in your body before you feel it in your mind.
  • You're not looking for more information. You're looking for a real shift.
  • You want to do this alongside other people — not alone, not on an app.
  • You already have a practice but something is still missing — and you suspect it might be community.
